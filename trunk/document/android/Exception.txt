[Unnormal]
Waiting for Debugger
Application HunyDew (process com.airvine.hunydew) is waiting for the debugger to attach.
[解决]
Some devices will only let the debugger attach if the application has the android.permission.SET_DEBUG_APP permission set in its manifest file:

<manifest>
  <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.SET_DEBUG_APP"></uses-permission>
</manifest>

[Failed]
Failed to parse the output of 'adb version'
[解决]
启动eclipse时，出现标题【Failed to parse the output of 'adb version'】的错误，经尝试，发现环境变量的配置不正确。
(1)设置Android路径
新建一个“系统变量”，在“变量名”中填Android_Home（大小写没有问题），在“变量值”加填SDK的根目录
(如：F:\android-sdk-windows-1.6_r1。）

(2)设置PATH路径

“编辑”PATH变量，在“变量值”后面加上;%Android_Home%\tools，注意前面的分号。
如果tools文件夹和platform-tools文件夹同时存在，建议也把%Android_Home%\platform-tools添加上。  

[Unnormal]
当设置 android:screenOrientation = "landscape"时
即使android:configChanges="orientation|keyboardHidden"
在部分机型上，还会出现锁屏调用onDestroy->onCreate的问题（点击HOME键无此问题）
[解决]
In the Manifest, you can set the screenOrientation to landscape. It would look something like this in the XML:

<activity android:name="MyActivity"
android:screenOrientation="landscape"
android:configChanges="keyboardHidden|orientation|screenSize">
...
</activity>

Where MyActivity is the one you want to stay in landscape.
The android:configChanges=... line prevents onResume(), onPause() from being called when the screen is rotated. 
Without this line, the rotation will stay as you requested but the calls will still be made.
Note: keyboardHidden and orientation are required for < Android 3.2 (API level 13), 
and all three options are required 3.2 or above, not just orientation.


[Exception]
Android中引入第三方Jar包的方法(java.lang.NoClassDefFoundError解决办法)
[解决]
1、在工程下新建lib文件夹，将需要的第三方包拷贝进来。
2、将引用的第三方包，添加进工作的build path。
3、（关键的一步）将lib设为源文件夹。如果不设置，则程序编译可以通过，但运行的时候，会报：java.lang.NoClassDefFoundError
